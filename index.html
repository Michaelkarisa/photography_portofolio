About

Light is the medium.
Truth is the subject.

I started Inferno Photography in 2017 with a secondhand DSLR and a single conviction: that every person, place, and passing moment holds a frame worth preserving. Since then, I've shot across the Kenyan coast, documented intimate family stories, and collaborated with brands that believe in the power of visual honesty.

My approach is unhurried and observational. I don't direct so much as I witness—creating space for genuine moments to emerge, then catching them before they disappear. The name Inferno isn't about spectacle. It's about intensity of attention.

Artistic philosophy: Every image should feel like something someone lived, not something someone posed for.

Sony Alpha Lightroom Capture One Film Photography Natural Light Studio Lighting Drone Aerial Post-Production

Clients include: Independent brands, NGOs, hospitality groups, private families, editorial publications across the Kenyan coast and beyond.
